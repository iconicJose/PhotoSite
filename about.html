About

Jose Calderon is a photographer and college student based in Chicago, Illinois.

He spends most of his time working in and around the Midwest, including Wisconsin, Illinois, and Iowa, and has also traveled to Mexico to photograph different landscapes and environments.

This site is intended as a straightforward record of those places—cliffs, caves, water, and city scenes—as they appear through the lens at the time they were made.
